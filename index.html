Rules
You are allowed to harvest resources from Farm Bases you are assigned as given below. Do not attack if durability is under 50%. Harvesting other's farms or harvesting without signing up will have consequences.
Dates
Alpha Harvest : 2nd Week of every month
Beta Harvest : 4th Week of every month
Time
Monday 00:00 to Friday 23:59, Server time
Eligibility
You are eligible if you are an PuB Alliance member with base level 16+ and you come under any one of the follow:
  1. No farm accounts
  2. No time for managing farm accounts
  3. Unable to manage farm accounts
